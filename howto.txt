Instruction:

In order to play the game,
1) Place ships in player field.
  - Click one of the spot in the field,
    then window pops up and you can choose direction and ship.
2) After place the ship, you need to find the .battle file 
   that is the coordinate that you have to guess.
3) After all this steps are done, you can click start button to play game.
4) You can not click start button when you are not ready to play.
  - placing all ships in player area and select .battle file.
5) Click one of the coordinate in computer area then computer will also guess.
6) All ship are different size and they look like vertical or horizontal line.
7) After whoever wins, you can play the game again.

Enjoy the battle ship!
